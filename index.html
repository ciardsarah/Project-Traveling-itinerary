Tsukiji Market

The Tuskiji Market is Tokyo's best market. There you can encounter different kinds of Japanese delicacies from sushi to bento boxes, everything can be found there!

The Izu Penninsula: A Ryokan

The Izu Penninsula is a home to pristine forests, rugged coastlines, and dozens of top-quality ryokans. At the Ryokans you can relax and unwind in the traditional Japanese onsens. The penninsula is also not too far from Tokyo and easy to reach.

Naoshima

Naoshima is a wonderful destination where art and and architecture-lovers will feel at home. The majestic scenery, charming fishing villages, and cutting-edge modern art and architecture all blend to create a wonderful combination.

The Philosopher's Path

Tetsugaku No Michi or The Philosopher's Path is a trail that followsa cherry-tree-lined canal. This charming pedestrian path is perfect for little stroll to exercise your legs while enjoying fresh air and a picturesque scenery

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine is an important shrine famous for its torii gates. The shrine is one of several shrines dedicated to Shinto the God of rice. At the very back of the shrine's main grounds is the entrance to the torii gate-covered hiking trail.
